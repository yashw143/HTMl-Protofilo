Instructions

Apart from flavor, cinnamon is also used in commercial baking to impart a specific color to a product.
“For certain purposes, it may be desirable to give a baked product high cinnamon colouring and yet only relatively mild cinnamon flavouring,”
write J. Thomas and P. P. Duethi in Handbook of Herbs and Spices.
